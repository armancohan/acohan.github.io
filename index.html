I am a Research Scientist at Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2). My broad research interests include natural language processing, information retrieval and their applications in specialized domains such as scientific publications and medical data. I received my PhD in computer science at Georgetown University in May 2018 where I worked with Nazli Goharian. You can check out my full CV here.
