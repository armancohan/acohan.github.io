Arman Cohan

I am a Research Scientist at Allen Institute for AI (AI2) and an Affiliate Assistant Professor at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington. My research is primarily focused on developing general Natural Language Processing capabilities to address information overload. These include core document-level representation learning and language modeling methods, natural language generation and summarization, and methods for improved discovery. I received my PhD in computer science at Georgetown University in May 2018 where I worked with Nazli Goharian.

For most recent list of publications please refer to my Google Scholar page.
